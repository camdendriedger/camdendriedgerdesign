ABOUT

 NØXO

INTRODUCTION

Samuel Vautour, aka NØXO, is a Kitchener-based R&B / rap artist. Although currently working in the GTA, Sam has roots in Vancouver where he was born, from where he moved to Kitchener as a child. Growing up, Sam enjoyed listening to Wayne and Akon.

MUSIC

Sam's music could be categorized into trap, R&B, hip hop, and hints of lofi. Currently, he enjoys listening to and pulls inspiration from Flame, Zoocci Coke Dope, Tory Lanez, J. Cole, and many more.

PUBLICITY
